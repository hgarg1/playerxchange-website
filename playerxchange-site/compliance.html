Compliance is non-negotiable

PlayerXChange runs compliance workflows across every transaction so athletes and athletic departments stay protected.

Not a security

PlayerXChange collectibles grant access to perks and community utility. They do not represent equity, investment contracts, or financial returns. Pricing and liquidity are market-driven and not guaranteed.

KYC / AML

We run tiered Know-Your-Customer and anti-money laundering checks during onboarding. Partner banks provide watchlist screening, sanctions checks, and case management.

Geofencing

Jurisdictional rules differ. We geofence by IP and wallet metadata to honor state or conference-specific NIL policies, with manual override tools for compliance directors.

Audit logs

Every disclosure, signature, and perk redemption is timestamped with immutable logs. Exports feed directly into university compliance portals.
