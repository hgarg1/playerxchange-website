The complete PlayerXChange roadmap

PlayerXChange lets fans buy and trade compliant NIL shares while athletes earn upfront and lifetime royalties. Explore the steps, safeguards, and technology that power every drop.

Quick summary

PlayerXChange ties every share to an athlete’s Name, Image, and Likeness—not equity in a person. Fans fund their wallets in USD, mint limited drops, and unlock utility while athletes stay NCAA/NAIA compliant with automated royalties and reporting.

Who it’s for

Fans

  • Deeper connections through drops, raffles, and gated content.
  • Clear perk tiers based on percentage owned or share count.
  • Marketplace access to trade, bid, and hold long term.

Athletes

  • Compliant NIL income with transparent supply, price, and royalty splits.
  • Dashboards for payouts, taxes, and perk fulfillment workflows.
  • Controls to pause trading during sensitive periods.

Teams & schools

  • Policy-aware onboarding with disclosure tooling for compliance offices.
  • Safe on-ramp to NIL monetization that respects athlete well-being.
  • Immutable records of mints, trades, royalties, and approvals.

The flow at a glance

Step 01

Discover

Browse verified athlete profiles, see supply, drop timers, and perk plans before you commit.

Step 02

Verify & create account

Complete KYC and enable two-factor authentication to unlock minting, trading, and messaging.

Step 03

Fund wallet

Add funds via card, ACH, or on-ramp. Balances stay denominated in USD while blockchain rails run behind the scenes.

Step 04

Buy shares (mint)

Secure limited shares during drops. Smart contracts lock quantity, pricing, and royalty percentages.

Step 05

Use your perks

Redeem meet-and-greets, AMAs, seating upgrades, and merch codes based on your tier.

Step 06

Trade or hold

List shares on the marketplace, accept bids, or hold for future perks as supply and demand shift.

Step 07

Royalties to athletes

Every secondary sale routes royalties back to the athlete automatically—forever.

Compliance, safety & safeguards

Compliance & safety

  • NCAA/NAIA aware workflows and disclosure tooling for compliance officers.
  • No pay-for-play or performance-contingent compensation—utility and access only.
  • Age, geography, and school-policy filters to prevent restricted activity.
  • Full audit trails of mints, trades, royalties, and disclosures for review.
  • KYC/AML vendor integrations with suspicious activity monitoring.

Liquidity & risk safeguards

  • Managed liquidity pools may provide instant buy-backs for fractional shares during beta.
  • Pause or retire collections with pathways for refunds or swaps per posted policy.
  • Clear risk disclosures that prices can go down—this isn’t an investment vehicle.
  • Anti-manipulation monitoring and circuit-breaker policies to protect market integrity.

Technology, pricing & royalties

Smart contracts & technology

  • Each drop is governed by a smart contract that enforces supply, royalties, and transfer rules.
  • Custodial wallets by default for ease of use with optional self-custody export.
  • USD-first experience with blockchain transparency available on-chain.
  • Public, auditable contracts so compliance and fans can verify activity.

Pricing, fees & royalties

  • Primary prices are set with athletes and advisors for every drop.
  • Marketplace fees are shown before checkout—no hidden costs.
  • Athlete royalties on every secondary sale are routed automatically.
  • Withdrawals to linked bank accounts follow standard compliance checks.

Perks, fulfillment & support

Perks & fulfillment

  • Perks fulfilled via in-app tickets, codes, or verified guest lists—no screenshots required.
  • Deadlines, blackout windows, and fair-use rules maintain sustainable experiences.
  • Missed or canceled events trigger alternate perks or credits as outlined on drop pages.

Accessibility & support

  • Email and chat support during business hours with 24/7 abuse reporting.
  • Accessibility standards (WCAG) for color contrast, keyboard navigation, and screen readers.
  • In-app tutorials, tooltips, and a Learn hub to onboard new users.

Responsible use & important disclaimers

  • Shares provide NIL utility and digital access only; they are not securities or equity in a person.
  • Market prices are driven by demand, and perks can change; past popularity is not a guarantee.
  • All users must comply with local laws, school rules, and platform policies.
  • Taxes may apply to athlete earnings and certain user rewards—consult a tax professional.
