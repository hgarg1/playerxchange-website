The complete PlayerXChange roadmap

PlayerXChange lets fans buy and trade compliant NIL shares while athletes earn upfront and lifetime royalties. Explore the steps, safeguards, and technology that power every drop.

Quick summary

PlayerXChange ties every share to an athlete's Name, Image, and Likeness—not equity in a person. Fans fund their wallets in USD, mint limited drops, and unlock utility while athletes stay NCAA/NAIA compliant with automated royalties and reporting.

Who it's for

Fans

  • Deeper connections through drops, raffles, and gated content.
  • Clear perk tiers based on percentage owned or share count.
  • Marketplace access to trade, bid, and hold long term.

Athletes

  • Compliant NIL income with transparent supply, price, and royalty splits.
  • Dashboards for payouts, taxes, and perk fulfillment workflows.
  • Controls to pause trading during sensitive periods.

Teams & schools

  • Policy-aware onboarding with disclosure tooling for compliance offices.
  • Safe on-ramp to NIL monetization that respects athlete well-being.
  • Immutable records of mints, trades, royalties, and approvals.

The flow at a glance

Step 01

Discover

Browse verified athlete profiles, see supply, drop timers, and perk plans before you commit.

Step 02

Verify & create account

Complete KYC and enable two-factor authentication to unlock minting, trading, and messaging.

Step 03

Fund wallet

Add funds via card, ACH, or on-ramp. Balances stay denominated in USD while blockchain rails run behind the scenes.

Step 04

Buy shares (mint)

Secure limited shares during drops. Smart contracts lock quantity, pricing, and royalty percentages.

Step 05

Use your perks

Redeem meet-and-greets, AMAs, seating upgrades, and merch codes based on your tier.

Step 06

Trade or hold

List shares on the marketplace, accept bids, or hold for future perks as supply and demand shift.

Step 07

Royalties to athletes

Every secondary sale routes royalties back to the athlete automatically—forever.

What athletes get

  • Policy-aligned controls to pause trading during injuries or transfers.
  • Brand-safe moderation that protects reputation and well-being.
